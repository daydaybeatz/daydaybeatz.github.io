* item(hunters_bow)
* set(gold,+5)

enemy:bandit
enemy:bandit

* flag(bandits_dealt,true)
* quest(debt_to_bandits,complete)
* item(heavy_boots)
* set(gold,+8)
* room(broken_hills)

* say(The bandits leave you bruised.)
* room(courtyard)

@combat corrupted_shrine
enemy:corrupted
fleeable:false

on_win:
* set(reputation,+1)
* room(courtyard)

on_lose:
* lose

@room courtyard
Brookhaven Crossing opens before you.
A stone well rests near the center.
A shrine flickers dimly.
A bridge gate leads outward.

- Take in the surroundings
* if(!flag:visited_courtyard) flag(visited_courtyard,true)
* say(The village feels calm, but tense.)

- Speak with Elda
* room(elda)

- Visit Marn’s trade post
* room(marn)

- Check the notice board
* room(notice)

- Head to Riverside Hamlet
* room(riverside)

- Enter Old Growth Woods
* room(woods)

- Climb toward Broken Hills
* room(broken_hills)

- Pray at the shrine
!chance
* 70% say(The air feels heavy.)
* 30% combat(corrupted_shrine)

@room notice
The board is cluttered with worn postings.

- Accept “Missing Traps”
* quest(missing_traps,start,Clear wolves near the river.)

- Accept “Water Runs Dark”
* quest(water_runs_dark,start,Investigate the flooded cellar.)

- Accept “Debt to Bandits”
* quest(debt_to_bandits,start,Deal with bandits in the hills.)

- Accept “The Runaway”
* quest(the_runaway,start,Find Lysa near the woods.)

- Step away
* room(courtyard)

@npc elda
Elda watches you closely.

- Ask about the village
* say(Brookhaven survives by balance, not truth.)

- Ask about the woods
* say(The roots run deep. Best not disturb them.)

- Press her for answers
* if(reputation>=1) say(Some doors stay buried for a reason.)

- Leave
* room(courtyard)

@npc marn
Marn grins from behind his stall.

- Ask about supplies
* say(Potions and tools cost more when trouble brews.)

- Sell pelts
* if(flag:wolves_cleared) set(gold,+6)
* say(Marn counts out the coins.)

- Ask about rumors
* say(Bandits, beasts, and worse under the trees.)

- Leave
* room(courtyard)

@room riverside
Water flows past a small hamlet.
A mill creaks slowly.

- Inspect the riverbank
!chance
* 60% combat(wolves_river)
* 40% say(Tracks fade into the mud.)

- Visit the abandoned shack
* room(shack)

- Return to the crossing
* room(courtyard)

@room shack
The shack smells of rot.

- Search inside
!chance
* 50% set(health,-10)
* 50% say(Nothing but mold.)

- Leave the shack
* room(riverside)

@room woods
Ancient trees loom overhead.

- Search for Lysa
* room(lysa)

- Approach the fallen watchtower
* room(watchtower)

- Return
* room(courtyard)

@npc lysa
Lysa steps back nervously.

- Convince her to return
* set(reputation,+1)
* quest(the_runaway,complete)
* room(courtyard)

- Help her hide
* flag(lysa_hidden,true)
* set(reputation,-1)
* quest(the_runaway,complete)
* room(courtyard)

- Leave her be
* room(woods)

@room watchtower
Roots twist through broken stone.

- Examine the roots
* if(flag:vault_unsealed) say(The vault stands open.)
* if(!flag:vault_unsealed) say(Something lies buried here.)

- Try to unseal the stone
* if(reputation>=1) flag(vault_unsealed,true)
* if(reputation>=1) quest(stone_beneath_roots,complete)
* if(reputation>=1) room(vault)
* if(reputation<1) say(The stone won’t budge.)

- Step away
* room(woods)

@room broken_hills
Wind howls across shattered rock.

- Approach the bandit lookout
!chance
* 50% combat(bandit_fight)
* 50% say(The lookout is empty.)

- Pay off the bandits
* if(gold>=10) set(gold,-10)
* if(gold>=10) flag(bandits_dealt,true)
* if(gold>=10) quest(debt_to_bandits,complete)
* if(gold>=10) say(The bandits leave you alone.)
* if(gold<10) say(Not enough gold.)

- Return
* room(courtyard)

@room vault
Ancient machinery hums beneath twisted roots.
The truth of Brookhaven lies below.

- Step into the vault
* win
